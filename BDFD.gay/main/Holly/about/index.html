About Holly

I'm genderfluid, and I mix between Demiboy Non-Binary, Female, and Male.
If you're wondering, I was born a female, but I most definitely do not feeling like I am one.
I'm mainly Demiboy Non-Binary, meaning I'm Non-Binary and partially identity as Male, at the same time.
I also switch to Female. It's less common to see me when having a feminine day, though there's a high chance, depending on who your are, that you'll see one of my Female moments.
I very rarely switch to Male for an unknown reason. The only masculine moments I have are during moments of uncontrollable anger, and the days of feeling masculine are very uncommon.

When using my pronouns, feel free to ask me what I currently identify, or check my About Me section on Discord. I'll try to update it regularly with my chosen name and pronouns.
When using my pronouns to talk about an event that happened while I was using different pronouns to those used during the present, feel free to use either those I used during the event, or those I am using at the time of the conversation. I don't mind.
